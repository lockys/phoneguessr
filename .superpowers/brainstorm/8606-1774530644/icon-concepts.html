App Icon Concept

Which direction feels right for PhoneGuessr? (app icon: 512×512, shown at various sizes)

A — Phone + Question Mark

Classic, instantly readable. Dark navy background, white phone outline, amber "?" on screen. Clean at all sizes.

B — Phone + Magnifying Glass

Blue-to-purple gradient, white phone, gold magnifying glass overlapping. Communicates "inspect & identify". More dynamic.

C — Cropped Phone + Guess Dots

Teal-to-blue gradient, partially cropped phone silhouette (mirrors the game mechanic), gold/grey dots representing guesses remaining.

D — Phone + Eye

Purple-to-pink gradient, phone outline with an eye on the screen. "Look closely and identify." Bold and distinctive.
