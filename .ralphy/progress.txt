### Key design decisions
1. No separate streaks table — derive from results table to avoid sync issues
2. Hint penalty applied at result submission time, not in hint endpoint
3. Phone metadata columns are nullable for backward compatibility
4. Share card is client-only — no API changes needed
5. Score formula: `elapsed_seconds + (wrong_guesses × 10) + (hints_used × 15)`
